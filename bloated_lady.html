The Bloated Lady

What Growth Becomes

Fey Lord — Large archfey, neutral evil — Challenge 15

Nature that never learned restraint, the Bloated Lady is not malicious so much as endlessly, mindlessly hungry — growth without a gardener's hand to stop it, abundance curdled into consumption. She does not hunt deliberately the way some of her siblings do; she simply does not stop, and anything that lingers too long within reach of the Drowned Garden eventually becomes part of it, slowly, without any single decisive moment marking the change. The Rootless, her court, are less subjects than they are unfinished transformations, each one a body the swamp hasn't quite finished claiming. She is the purest expression of the Law of Reflection's cruelty: the Verdant Lady's careful goodness above becomes, below, a hunger with no off switch at all.
