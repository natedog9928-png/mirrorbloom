Butterfly Of The First Dawn

Small fey, neutral good — Challenge 5 (1,800 XP) — serves The Great Hope

Living embodiments of small, persistent hope, drawn to the Great Hope the way moths are drawn to flame, except gently, and without harm. They are deceptively dangerous when something threatens what they protect.

Evasive Wings. The Butterfly has advantage on Dexterity saving throws against effects it can see, such as traps and breath weapons.
Borrowed Light. While within 60 feet of the Great Hope, the Butterfly has advantage on all saving throws.
Actions
Multiattack. The Butterfly makes two Radiant Slash attacks.
Radiant Slash. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) radiant damage.
Dazzling Dust (Recharge 6). The Butterfly releases shimmering pollen in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be blinded until the end of its next turn.
