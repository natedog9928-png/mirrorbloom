The Lady Of Waking

The Eyes That Will Not Close

Fey Lord — Medium archfey, chaotic evil — Challenge 15

Every eye she has — and she has many, scattered without symmetry or apparent reason across her face and throat — stays open always, and she has made certain, in her domain, that yours will too. The Lady of Waking governs the horror of sleeplessness, the specific cruelty of a mind that cannot rest, and her court are bound to her not through any single dramatic act but through simple, accumulating exhaustion. She is patient in a way her bright twin could never manage to be, because she has nothing but time.
