Spreading Rot. A creature hit by the Bloated Lady's Fungal Maw or that starts its turn within 10 feet of her gains 1 level of Bloom Sickness (see below) unless it succeeds on a DC 19 Constitution saving throw. Bloom Sickness levels persist until the creature finishes a long rest, and the saving throw DC does not decrease as levels accumulate. Bloom Sickness (cumulative): 1: The creature's skin sprouts faint fungal patches. No mechanical effect yet, but the creature can be magically detected as infected. 2: The creature has disadvantage on Constitution saving throws. 3: The creature's speed is reduced by 10 feet as growths stiffen its joints. 4: The creature takes 9 (2d8) poison damage at the start of each of its turns. 5: The creature is poisoned and incapacitated as the infection fully takes root, until magically cured (lesser restoration or similar removes 2 levels).
Never Sated. The Bloated Lady has advantage on Constitution saving throws made to maintain concentration, and her size and hunger mean she cannot be reduced below 1 hit point by a single attack unless that attack deals damage equal to or greater than half her hit point maximum.
Innate Spellcasting. The Bloated Lady's spellcasting ability is Constitution (spell save DC 20). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: ray of sickness, stinking cloud 3/day each: contagion 1/day each: insect plague
Legendary Resistance (2/Day). If the Bloated Lady fails a saving throw, she can choose to succeed instead.
Actions
Multiattack. The Bloated Lady makes two Fungal Maw attacks.
Fungal Maw. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 23 (4d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) poison damage. See Spreading Rot.
Spore Cloud (Recharge 5–6). The Bloated Lady releases a 20-foot-radius cloud of choking spores centered on herself. Each creature in the area must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) poison damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. Whether or not the save succeeds, each creature in the area gains 1 level of Bloom Sickness.
Consuming Embrace. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one Medium or smaller creature. Hit: 16 (2d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 19) and restrained until the grapple ends. While grappled, the target automatically gains 1 level of Bloom Sickness at the start of each of its turns, no save.
The Drowned Garden Bleeds Through (Recharge 6). The Bloated Lady reaches for the boundary between Mirrorbloom and her realm and tears it thin. The floor softens and darkens within a 30-foot radius centered on her, turning to wet black peat that swallows footsteps, standing water seeping up between boards or stones as though the room itself is sweating. This transformation lasts until the end of the encounter, and the area becomes difficult terrain that does not impede the Bloated Lady. A Rotblossom Crawler surfaces from the muck and acts on the Bloated Lady's initiative count. Every creature in the area, including any of her allies, must immediately succeed on a DC 19 Constitution saving throw or gain 1 level of Bloom Sickness, and the swamp does not recede for the rest of the encounter.
Legendary Actions

The Bloated Lady can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. She regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.

Lurch. The Bloated Lady moves up to half her speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
Bite. The Bloated Lady makes one Fungal Maw attack.
Fester (Costs 2 Actions). One creature within 60 feet of the Bloated Lady that already has at least 1 level of Bloom Sickness must succeed on a DC 19 Constitution saving throw or gain 1 additional level.
