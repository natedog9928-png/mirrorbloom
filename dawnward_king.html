The Dawnward King

First Light

Fey Lord — Huge archfey, chaotic good — Challenge 15

Born in the instant the Breaking occurred — the very first moment of the new, split world — the Dawnward King has lived every day since as though it were that same first moment, because to him, it always is. He retains no memory from one sunrise to the next; the Unworn Throne is attended each morning by a court he is meeting, as far as he knows, for the very first time, regardless of how many centuries they've actually served him. This is not cruelty and not tragedy, exactly — the Dawnward King is, by every account, profoundly and consistently joyful, incapable of holding a grudge, a wound, or a plan for longer than a single day allows. His shadow, the Duskward King, carries the memory he cannot, and the two have never spoken, as far as anyone can determine, in all the time since the Breaking.
