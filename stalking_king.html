The Stalking King

The Chase That Never Ends

Fey Lord — Large archfey, chaotic evil — Challenge 15

Where his bright twin's hunt is a contest both sides can win, the Stalking King's hunt was never fair and was never meant to end. Once marked as his quarry, a creature cannot meaningfully escape. His horn, identical in every visible respect to the Hunter King's, makes no sound; whatever capacity for honorable mercy exists in his bright counterpart was, in the Breaking, left entirely on the other side of the split.
