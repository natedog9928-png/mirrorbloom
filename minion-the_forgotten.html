The Forgotten

Medium fey, lawful evil — Challenge 8 (3,900 XP) — serves The Great Despair

Once-mortal servants whose names and faces the Great Despair has taken in trade for power. They wear blank, smooth ovals where their features should be, and they serve without complaint because they no longer remember they had a choice. The Great Despair's lair typically holds 1d4+1 of them.

Faceless. The Forgotten has no facial features and is immune to any effect that would require seeing or targeting a face, including gaze attacks that rely on eye contact.
Shared Sorrow. Whenever the Great Despair uses Despair Feeds within 60 feet of a Forgotten, that Forgotten also regains 5 hit points.
Actions
Multiattack. The Forgotten makes two Hollow Blade attacks.
Hollow Blade. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d10 + 4) slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) psychic damage.
Echo of a Name (1/Day). The Forgotten speaks a single word in a voice that isn't its own — the last name it remembers being its own. Each creature within 10 feet that hears it must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened until the end of the Forgotten's next turn.
