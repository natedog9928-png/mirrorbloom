Half-Formed. The Lady of Dreaming's body is barely corporeal. She has advantage on Dexterity saving throws, and whenever a creature attacks her, it has disadvantage on the attack roll unless the attacker has truesight or the Lady of Dreaming has used Dream Logic against it this round (see below).
Dream Logic. At the start of each of her turns, the Lady of Dreaming alters the rules of reality in a 60-foot radius around herself by rolling 1d6 and applying the result until the start of her next turn: 1 — Falling Stairs: difficult terrain shifts randomly; at the start of each creature's turn in the area, the DM may move it 10 feet in a random direction (no action required, no attack of opportunity provoked). 2 — Wrong Door: any creature that takes the Dash action in the area instead remains in place, having gone nowhere. 3 — Heavy Air: all creatures in the area have disadvantage on Strength and Dexterity checks, as though moving through water. 4 — Borrowed Time: initiative order is rerolled for all creatures in the area at the start of the Lady of Dreaming's next turn. 5 — Familiar Stranger: each creature in the area must succeed on a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or treat the nearest ally as unrecognizable for 1 round, unable to take the Help action or coordinate. 6 — Clarity: no effect this round; even dreams have calm moments.
Unbound by Geometry. The Lady of Dreaming can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain, and she takes no damage if she ends her turn inside a solid object, instead being harmlessly shunted to the nearest open space.
Innate Spellcasting. The Lady of Dreaming's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 19). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: sleep, minor illusion, silent image 3/day each: dream, hypnotic pattern, phantasmal force 1/day each: weird
Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If the Lady of Dreaming fails a saving throw, she can choose to succeed instead.
Actions
Multiattack. The Lady of Dreaming makes two Drifting Touch attacks.
Drifting Touch. Melee Spell Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (4d8 + 4) psychic damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or be unable to distinguish illusion from reality until the end of its next turn, treating all difficult terrain it can see as impassable and vice versa.
Half-Remembered Door (Recharge 5–6). The Lady of Dreaming opens a door that shouldn't exist beneath up to three creatures she can see within 60 feet. Each target must succeed on a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or fall through, taking 27 (6d8) psychic damage and being teleported to a random unoccupied space within 30 feet of its original location, on a failure. On a success, a target takes half damage and isn't teleported.
The Deep Dream Bleeds Through (Recharge 6). The Lady of Dreaming reaches for the boundary between Mirrorbloom and her realm and tears it thin. Within a 30-foot radius centered on her, surfaces lose their certainty — floor and ceiling swap which one feels solid, colors drift loose from their shapes, and the whole area goes soft at the edges like ink dissolving in water. This transformation lasts until the end of the encounter. A Wandering Notion seeps out of the haze and acts on the Lady of Dreaming's initiative count. Allies in the area can move freely through difficult terrain and through other creatures' spaces. Enemies in the area, however, have their movement direction randomized slightly each turn — at the start of an enemy's turn, the DM secretly shifts its intended destination up to 10 feet off course, the same uncontrolled drift that defines her Dream Logic.
Legendary Actions

The Lady of Dreaming can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. She regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.

Drift. The Lady of Dreaming flies up to half her speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
Touch. The Lady of Dreaming makes one Drifting Touch attack.
Reroll the Dream (Costs 2 Actions). The Lady of Dreaming immediately re-rolls her Dream Logic effect for the current round.
