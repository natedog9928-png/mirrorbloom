The Lady Of Dreaming

The Shape Sleep Takes

Fey Lord — Large archfey, chaotic neutral — Challenge 15

She is barely a person at all, even by the standards of the Fey Lords — a drift of starlight and color where a body should be, experienced more than seen, known through dreams rather than direct encounter. The Lady of Dreaming governs sleep and imagination across all of Wonderland, and her presence in a sleeping mind is gentle almost without exception; nightmares, properly speaking, belong to her shadow twin, not to her. She is, perhaps, the Fey Lord least concerned with Wonderland's politics, the Law of Reflection, or anything beyond the immediate, soft, half-formed moment she happens to currently be.
