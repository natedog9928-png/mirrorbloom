Cannot Look Away. Any creature that ends its turn within 60 feet of the Lady of Waking without having taken the Attack action against her, cast a spell targeting her, or moved at least 10 feet further from her since the start of its turn must succeed on a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or take 18 (4d8) psychic damage, as her unblinking attention finds anything that tries to hide from it, including inaction itself.
Never Sleeps. The Lady of Waking does not sleep, and creatures within 60 feet of her cannot benefit from a short or long rest while she remains conscious and aware of their presence.
Too Many Eyes. The Lady of Waking cannot be surprised, has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks, and cannot be flanked; attackers do not gain advantage from being on opposite sides of her.
Unblinking. The Lady of Waking has advantage on saving throws against being blinded, charmed, or put to sleep by magical means, and any effect that would close her eyes against her will simply fails.
Innate Spellcasting. The Lady of Waking's spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 19). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: tasha's hideous laughter (reflavored as forced wakefulness), false vision 3/day each: synaptic static 1/day each: eyebite
Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If the Lady of Waking fails a saving throw, she can choose to succeed instead.
Actions
Multiattack. The Lady of Waking makes two Unblinking Grasp attacks.
Unblinking Grasp. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (3d8 + 6) psychic damage, and the target can't regain hit points until the end of its next turn, certain that closing its eyes even briefly would be unbearable.
Fixed Gaze (Recharge 5–6). The Lady of Waking forces up to three creatures she can see within 30 feet to make a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, a target is paralyzed until the end of its next turn, eyes locked open, unable to look away from her even to defend itself.
Glitch in the Pattern. The Lady of Waking targets one creature she can see within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 19 Intelligence saving throw or perceive a phantom extra limb, eye, or shadow attached to an ally of its choice within 30 feet, gaining disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than that ally until the end of its next turn.
The Unblinking Room Bleeds Through (Recharge 6). The Lady of Waking reaches for the boundary between Mirrorbloom and her realm and tears it thin. Within a 30-foot radius centered on her, every shadow burns away at once under a flat, sourceless light, color draining out of every surface until everything is the same exhausted white, a faint hum of somewhere-else audible underneath the silence. This transformation lasts until the end of the encounter. A Sleepless Watcher steps through the glare and acts on the Lady of Waking's initiative count. While the area persists, no creature within it can regain hit points from any short-rest-style feature that requires spending a moment to recover mid-combat, and her Cannot Look Away trait's range extends to cover the entire transformed area rather than just 60 feet of her.
Legendary Actions

The Lady of Waking can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. She regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.

Step. The Lady of Waking moves up to half her speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
Grasp. The Lady of Waking makes one Unblinking Grasp attack.
Another Eye Opens (Costs 2 Actions). The Lady of Waking forces one creature within 60 feet to immediately attempt its Cannot Look Away saving throw, regardless of what it has or hasn't done this turn.
