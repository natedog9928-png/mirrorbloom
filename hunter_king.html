The Hunter King

The Fair Chase

Fey Lord — Large archfey, neutral — Challenge 15

The hunt, to the Hunter King, has never been about the kill — it is about the chase itself, conducted with total honesty on both sides, hunter and prey equally capable of winning. His horn, sounded at the right moment, ends any pursuit honorably and absolutely; a creature that yields to the Hunter King is never pursued again. He is neither malicious nor truly safe, but the rules are real, consistently honored, and entirely without exception.
