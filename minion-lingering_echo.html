Lingering Echo

Medium undead, neutral evil — Challenge 4 (1,100 XP) — serves The Duskward King

Translucent remnants of those the Duskward King has outlasted, kept near him not out of cruelty but because he cannot let an ending go. They drift through his court in silence, replaying their final moments forever.

AC 13    HP 58 (9d8 + 18)    Speed 0 ft., fly 30 ft. (hover)

Resist acid, fire, lightning, thunder; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
Immune cold, necrotic, poison
Cond. Immune charmed, exhaustion, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained
Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
Languages understands the languages it knew in life but speaks only in fragments
Incorporeal Movement. The Echo can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes 5 (1d10) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.
Replay the End. Whenever the Echo takes damage, it has a 50% chance to instead replay the moment of its own death, becoming briefly intangible and immune to all damage from that hit.
Actions
Mournful Touch. Melee Spell Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d8 + 2) necrotic damage, and the target's hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken until the target finishes a long rest.
Echoing Wail (1/Day). Each creature within 20 feet of the Echo that can hear it must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened until the end of its next turn.
