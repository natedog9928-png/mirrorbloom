The Jester King

A Thousand Faces, Each One Borrowed

Fey Lord — Medium archfey, chaotic neutral — Challenge 15

No one has ever seen the Jester King's true face, including, it's whispered, the Jester King himself. He wears a new mask to every audience, every duel, every performance, each one beautiful, each one a deliberate choice rather than a deception meant to wound — his court, the Punchline, plays a strange and constant game of riddles and wit that occasionally tips, without warning, into genuine prophecy. He is chaotic but never cruel by intention; the harm he occasionally causes tends to be collateral, the unavoidable cost of a joke landing exactly where it needed to in order to be true.
