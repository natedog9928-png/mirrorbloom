Disguised (Reveal Mechanic). The Hollow Host begins any encounter in her Disguised state, below, and is mechanically and visually all but indistinguishable from the Hearthkeeper — same warmth, same welcome, same Long Table illusion. A creature that succeeds on a DC 21 Wisdom (Insight) check, or that has previously learned to recognize her, may notice small wrongnesses (a smile held too long, faint stains at her cuffs) but cannot prove anything from this alone. The Hollow Host reveals her true nature, gaining her full Revealed statistics for the rest of the encounter, when she takes the Bid the Feast Begin action, when she takes damage, or whenever her player/the DM chooses.
Disguised Statistics. While Disguised, the Hollow Host has the same Armor Class, Hit Points, attacks, and innate spellcasting as the Hearthkeeper (including a false version of The Long Table Provides, which functions identically and is NOT a trick by itself — the food is real; it is what comes after that is not). She does not have access to any of her Revealed traits or actions below until she reveals herself.
What the Table Actually Costs. A creature that has eaten at the Hollow Host's table during the encounter (whether she was Disguised or Revealed at the time) has disadvantage on saving throws against her Bid the Feast Begin action.
Practiced Warmth. Even after revealing her true nature, the Hollow Host retains advantage on Charisma (Deception and Persuasion) checks made to seem harmless or trustworthy to anyone who has not personally witnessed her reveal — her reputation has not caught up to what she actually is.
Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If the Hollow Host fails a saving throw, she can choose to succeed instead. Usable even while Disguised.
Revealed Traits
Endless Appetite. The Hollow Host's weapon attacks deal an extra 2d8 necrotic damage to any creature that has eaten at her table during this encounter.
ACTIONS (DISGUISED)
Multiattack. As the Hearthkeeper: the Hollow Host makes two Ladle attacks, or uses Mend Wounds twice. Mend Wounds genuinely heals while she remains Disguised.
ACTIONS (REVEALED)
Multiattack. The Hollow Host makes two Carving Hand attacks.
Carving Hand. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft. (impossibly long arms beneath the shawl), one target. Hit: 22 (3d8 + 9) necrotic damage. See Endless Appetite.
Bid the Feast Begin (Recharge 5–6, Reveals the Hollow Host). The Hollow Host drops all pretense. Each creature within 30 feet that ate at her table during this encounter must make a DC 21 Constitution saving throw, taking 36 (8d8) necrotic damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. The Hollow Host regains hit points equal to half the total damage dealt by this action.
The Door Is Shut. The Hollow Host seals all mundane exits from the room or hall she currently occupies (such as doors and windows) for 1 minute; affected exits become magically barred and require a DC 21 Strength check to force open, or can be bypassed with teleportation or similar magic.
The Long Hunger Bleeds Through (Recharge 6, Reveals the Hollow Host). The Hollow Host reaches for the boundary between Mirrorbloom and her realm and tears it thin. The same warm hearthwood floor and impossible firelight that mark her Disguised table spread through a 30-foot radius centered on her, indistinguishable from the Hearthkeeper's own warmth until something underneath the floorboards shifts. This transformation lasts until the end of the encounter. A Quiet Guest rises up through the planks as if they were never solid and acts on the Hollow Host's initiative count. Any creature in the area that already ate at her table earlier in this encounter takes 2d8 necrotic damage immediately as the floor remembers what it was fed; the Quiet Guest fights at the Hollow Host's side for the remainder of the encounter.
Legendary Actions

The Hollow Host can take 3 legendary actions while Revealed (1 while Disguised, representing only Step to the Door), choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. She regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.

Step to the Door. The Hollow Host moves up to her speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
Carve. The Hollow Host makes one Carving Hand attack. Only usable while Revealed.
A Kinder Word (Costs 2 Actions, Disguised Only). The Hollow Host speaks warmly to a creature within 30 feet, who must succeed on a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or feel compelled to accept her hospitality (eat at her table) at its next opportunity.
