The Hearthkeeper

The Open Door

Fey Lord — Medium archfey, neutral good — Challenge 15

Of every Fey Lord born from the Breaking, the Hearthkeeper is the one most genuinely, uncomplicatedly good — warmth and hospitality made into a person, the safest presence in all of Wonderland by a margin no scholar disputes. The Long Table feeds anyone who sits at it, asks nothing in return, and has never once, in any recorded instance, betrayed that promise. She knows what her kindness costs below. She continues setting the table anyway.
