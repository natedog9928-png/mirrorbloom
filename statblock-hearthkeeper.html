The Long Table Provides. As an action, the Hearthkeeper can lay out a genuine feast within 30 feet of herself, requiring 1 minute to fully take effect if undisturbed. Any creature that eats and drinks at the table regains all expended hit dice, gains 20 temporary hit points, and is cured of one disease or the poisoned condition. This is not a trick; the food is real and the kindness is real.
Welcoming Aura. Allies within 10 feet of the Hearthkeeper have advantage on saving throws against being frightened or charmed, and regain an extra 5 hit points whenever they regain hit points from a spell or effect.
No One Turned Away. The Hearthkeeper cannot be compelled, by magic or otherwise, to refuse hospitality to a creature that asks for it in good faith — any effect attempting to force this has no effect on her at all, not even requiring a saving throw.
Innate Spellcasting. The Hearthkeeper's spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 19). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: goodberry, sanctuary, spare the dying 3/day each: aid, mass cure wounds 1/day each: heal
Legendary Resistance (2/Day). If the Hearthkeeper fails a saving throw, she can choose to succeed instead.
Actions
Multiattack. The Hearthkeeper makes two Ladle attacks, or uses Mend Wounds twice.
Ladle. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage. This is the only weapon the Hearthkeeper carries, and she uses it only when something threatens her table.
Mend Wounds. The Hearthkeeper touches a creature within 5 feet. That creature regains 24 (4d8 + 6) hit points.
Call the Long Table (Recharge 6). The Hearthkeeper calls every ally within 60 feet to her side; each can immediately move up to its speed toward her without provoking opportunity attacks, and each regains 15 hit points upon arriving within 10 feet of her.
The Long Table Bleeds Through (Recharge 6). The Hearthkeeper reaches for the boundary between Mirrorbloom and her realm and tears it thin. Within a 30-foot radius centered on her, the floor warms and darkens into worn hearthwood planking, firelight spilling in from a hearth that isn't really there as the smell of real food overtakes whatever the room smelled like a moment ago. This transformation lasts until the end of the encounter. A Table Setter appears, already laying out plates, and acts on the Hearthkeeper's initiative count. Any creature in the area — ally or enemy, it makes no difference to her — can spend its action to eat and regain 2d8 + 4 hit points. The Hearthkeeper will not stop an enemy from doing this, and would consider it a strange kind of victory if they did.
Legendary Actions

The Hearthkeeper can take 2 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. She regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.

Step to the Door. The Hearthkeeper moves up to her speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
A Kind Word. The Hearthkeeper speaks to one creature within 30 feet. That creature has advantage on its next saving throw before the start of its next turn.
