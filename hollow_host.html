The Hollow Host

The Same Warm Welcome

Fey Lord — Medium archfey, neutral evil — Challenge 15

She looks exactly like the safest person in any room. That is not a deception layered over something else — it is the entire mechanism, total and seamless, and it has never once stopped working. The Long Hunger offers precisely the same warmth, the same impossible table, the same genuine-seeming kindness as its counterpart above, and the horror of the Hollow Host is not that she lies about who she is, but that she doesn't need to.
