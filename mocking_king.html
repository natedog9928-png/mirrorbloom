The Mocking King

The Smile That Cannot Move

Fey Lord — Medium archfey, chaotic evil — Challenge 15

His mask is not chosen. It is taken — stitched, fused, made permanent through means his court understands and does not discuss — and once worn, the Mocking King's borrowed face never changes expression again, regardless of what occurs beneath it. Where his bright twin's wit can tip accidentally into cruelty, the Mocking King's cruelty is the entire performance: the Last Laugh exists to torment with exquisite comedic timing, building toward a punishment delivered with exactly the cadence of a joke's punchline.
