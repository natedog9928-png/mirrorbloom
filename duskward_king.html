The Duskward King

Last Light

Fey Lord — Huge archfey, neutral evil — Challenge 15

He remembers everything. Every ending, every failure, every quiet defeat that has ever occurred since the Breaking split him free of his brighter twin — all of it remains with him, perfectly preserved, growing heavier with each passing year. The Throne of Ash is less a seat of power than a place where endings come to rest, and the Duskward King receives them all without complaint, because complaint would require believing things could have gone differently, and he has the memory to prove, definitively, in case after case, that they did not. His court — bound to him not by choice but by the simple, terrible fact of having failed greatly enough to be remembered — has slowly learned to find a strange comfort in being witnessed so completely, even by something this sorrowful.
