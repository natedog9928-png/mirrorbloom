The Verdant Lady

The Garden Unbroken

Fey Lord — Large archfey, neutral good — Challenge 15

Of the six Fey Lords born from the Breaking, the Verdant Lady is, by wide consensus, the gentlest, and the Greenbower she tends is the closest thing Wonderland has to a place entirely without cruelty. She is growth itself, made patient and deliberate — not wild, exactly, despite her domain, but tended, the way a garden is wild only within careful boundaries a gardener has chosen. She asks nothing of her visitors beyond genuine care in return for genuine abundance. It is worth noting, and rarely said aloud, that her gentleness is not innocence — the Verdant Lady knows exactly what her own goodness costs the world below her, and tends her garden anyway, because the alternative, in her own words, helps no one and grows nothing.
