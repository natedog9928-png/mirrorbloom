The Great Hope

She Who Does Not Know

God — Gargantuan fey, neutral good — Challenge 24

Before the Breaking, there was no Wonderland above and no Hollow Court below — there was only the Great Hope and the Great Despair, two halves of a single radiant whole, though no living thing remembers what that whole was called, or whether it had a name at all. Something happened between them. No record survives of what. The world split in the moment of their parting, and the Great Hope rose into the bright half, untouched by whatever grief caused the rupture, because she does not remember there was a rupture at all. She believes herself simply to be hope, entire and uncomplicated, and in a very real sense, she is right — the not-knowing is not a wound to her, only an absence shaped like one. She wanders the Unclouded and occasionally Wonderland itself, radiant and sincere, utterly unaware that somewhere beneath her, something that loved her now believes a crown was stolen from its head. The six Fey Lords, born from the Breaking itself, know the truth. None of them have ever told her.
