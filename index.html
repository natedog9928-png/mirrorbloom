A Homebrew D&D Setting

Solance ◼ Umbrasol

One flat world, split top and bottom like a reflection on still water. Everything beautiful above feeds everything terrible below. This is not a metaphor.

◕ Cosmology The Breaking, the Law of Reflection, how the world came to be split. ⌛ Timeline Ten thousand years of history across five ages, from the Primordial Era to today. ✦ Lore The gods, the Fey Lords, their courts and realms, and the empires of Solance. ✔ Gilded Goose The guild. Crockett's people. Find it, understand it, share it.

Beauty and goodness generated above directly feeds horror and evil below. It is not optional. It is not metaphorical. It is closer to a law of physics than a moral teaching.

Mirrorbloom is a living homebrew setting — this site updates as the world grows.
